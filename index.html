The Blue House Quintet is a five-piece gypsy jazz band based in Chapel Hill, North Carolina. The group consists of Jonathan Ng on violin and vocals, Nathan Huvard and Avery Linderman on guitar, Kevin Beardsley on bass, and Kendrick Smith on drums.

Originally founded as a swing dance band, the Blue House Quintet brings a touch of Jazz Manouche to tunes of many different eras, from traditional Django to bebop tunes, all while keeping things swingin' and tailored for your dancing pleasure.
